FAQs

What documents are required for a new Hathway Broadband connection?

To apply for a connection, you need to provide identity proof (Aadhaar card, PAN card, etc.) and address proof (utility bill, rent agreement, etc.).

How can I upgrade my Hathway plan?

You can upgrade your plan by contacting Hathway customer support or through their official website.

Are there any installation charges for new connections?

No, Hathway does not charge any installation fees for new broadband connections in Indore.

Which is the best Hathway plan for high-speed internet in Indore?

The premium 300 Mbps plan is ideal for seamless streaming, gaming, and work-from-home requirements in Indore.

What is the typical installation time for Hathway Broadband in Indore?

Installation usually takes 24-48 hours after submitting the required documents and payment.

Does Hathway offer any special plans or discounts for students?

Hathway periodically provides discounted plans for students. Check the website or contact customer service for the latest offers.

What should I do if I face frequent internet disconnections?

Check the router's power connection and settings. If the issue persists, contact Hathway customer care for troubleshooting or a service request.
